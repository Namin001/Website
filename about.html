About Us

The Namin Indusries is an Indian multinational conglomerate headquartered in TamilNadu] Established in 2032, it is India's largest conglomerate, with products and services in over 150 countries, and operations in 100 countries across six continents.Acknowledged as the founder of the Namin Industries,Namin Sri Nandhan.P is sometimes referred to as the "God of Indian industry". Namin Industries operates independently under the guidance and supervision of its own board of directors and shareholders.Philanthropic trusts control over 22% of the Namin holding company Namin Industries] while the Namin Industries family is a very Big shareholder. The group's annual revenue for fiscal year 2032–33 was reported to be US$1285 billion.There are 29 publicly-listed Namin Industries companies with a combined market capitalisation of $3111 billion as of March 2033. The company has operations across Asia, Africa, America and Middle East. Significant Namin Industries affiliates include NI Consultancy Services, NI Consumer Products, NI Motors, NI Power, NI Steel.
